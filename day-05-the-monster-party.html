It's Friday, so the monsters are having a PARTY! Scribble, Speedster, and Backwards Bob are all at Scribble's house. They need to make a sign for the party and write invitations.

But every time one of them tries to write, it goes wrong! Scribble's writing is too messy, Speedster writes too fast to read, and Bob writes everything backwards. They need YOU to do ALL the writing!

🧩 For parents

This is the Week 1 finale! It's meant to feel like a celebration. Let him laugh at all three failed attempts, then build excitement: "They need YOU — you're the only one who can save this party!" Keep energy high throughout.

What's special today

This is the Week 1 celebration! The energy should feel like a finale. All three monsters are together for the first time, the child is the hero who saves the party by being the only one who can write. Lean into it — voices, excitement, drama. "Without you, there's no sign! No invitations! The party is DOOMED!"

Phase 2 — Personal record attempt

Use the exact same 3 letters from Day 2. Copy the Day 2 times into the "DAY 2" circles first. Then re-time each line. If he improved: "NEW RECORD! The monsters are going WILD!" If not: "Still a great time. Those are tough letters — you'll crush them next week."

Phase 3 — The choice

Let him pick Option A (party sign) or Option B (invitation). Both are great. If he finishes one quickly and wants to do the other, let him! The drawing area is the reward — "Now draw what the party looks like with all three monsters." This should feel fun, not like work.

Day 5 Wins

  • He re-did the letter sprint (any result is fine)
  • He attempted the party words
  • He completed at least one challenge option
  • He had fun — this is the big one!
  • You have a Week 1 total to write on the card

Watch for

  • Disappointment if records didn't improve — reframe as "same time = consistent!"
  • Wanting to do both options perfectly — pick one, move on
  • Rushing the drawing — let him enjoy it if there's time
  • Going over 15 min — end on a high note, even mid-drawing
